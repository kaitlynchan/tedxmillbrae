What is TEDxMillsHighSchool ?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxMillsHighSchool, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxMillbrae event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.

Speakers at TEDX Mills High School!

Brannon Beliso is an 8th degree black belt and the owner of One Martial Arts in San Francisco and Millbrae. He is the creator of One Merit Badges, an international life-skills building system. Brannon presents workshops and seminars with the mindset that we can all always do and be better. He is committed to being a student for life and dedicated to serving others.

Michelle Cleere, PhD Bestselling author, Dr. Michelle Cleere has a PhD in Clinical Psychology and a Masters in Sport Psychology and works with elite athletes, Olympians, musicians, and professionals as a Premiere Cognitive Behavioral Coach. She lives in the San Francisco Bay Area and enjoys working with clients across the globe. Her passion is unlocking the power of the mind so that people can perform at their full potential. She works with youth and teenagers to help them learn to cope with anxiety and develop the confidence they need as athletes, musicians, and artists. She also currently teaches at John F. Kennedy University in the Sports Psychology Department. In a past life, she was a competitive triathlete and enjoyed her days coaching triathletes. Dr. Cleere believes that many of the challenges facing youth today can be addressed through fostering a relationship with the brain’s activity and training our mind to cope with life’s frustrations, downfalls, changes, and defeats. Simply put, she empowers others to improve brain functioning, and ultimately performance at their best.

Chris Spetzler Executive Director of the Decision Education Foundation, is passionate about bringing decision skills to young people. Chris has taught Decision Quality to educators through Stanford Center for Professional Development and to students through Stanford Pre-Collegiate Studies. Chris’ online course, Decision Skills: Power Tools to Build Your Life, has had tens of thousands participants since 2013. In addition to teaching Decision Skills, Chris supports educators and organizations in realizing the benefits of decision education. Before joining Decision Education Foundation, Chris was a consultant in the pharmaceutical industry, applying decision analytic principles to developing life-saving drugs. He completed an MA at Stanford Graduate School of Education and an MBA at Thunderbird, a top US business school in international management.

Mission: What is TED ?

TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world. For more information, visit www.ted.com.
